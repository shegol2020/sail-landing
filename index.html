Results of Sochi Race Week 2020 Regatta

Over a hundred participants gathered on the coast of Sochi to compete for the main prize. Both newcomers and experienced sailors took part in the competition.

Throughout the entire voyage, we were accompanied by sunshine and beautiful weather, with wind reaching up to 20 knots on some days. The regatta was excellently organized: 135 sailors, 7 crews, 9 participating countries, 4 race days, 3 divisions.

Participating Teams:

  • Vostok 35
  • Fortune Sailing
  • Siberia
  • Leviathan
  • Black Swan
  • White Pepper
  • Pearl

The champion of the Sochi Race Week 2020 regatta was the Fortune Sailing team, who narrowly missed victory last year but surpassed the nearest competitor - the "Vostok 35" crew - by 5 points this season.

We in the team are very close-knit and understand each other without words, I think that's why we won.

Congratulations to the winners, and we look forward to seeing all participants again at the autumn regattas!

Artem Kolesnikov 20.06.2021
